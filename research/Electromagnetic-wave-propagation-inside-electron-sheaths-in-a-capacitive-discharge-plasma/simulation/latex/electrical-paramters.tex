%Electron-neutral collision frequency:

\[
\nu_{en} = n_n \cdot \sigma_{en} \cdot v_e
\]

%Where the average electron thermal velocity is:

\[
v_e = \sqrt{\frac{8 k_B T_e}{\pi m_e}}
\]

%The normalized Maxwellian electron energy distribution function (EEDF) is:

\[
f(\varepsilon) = \frac{2}{\sqrt{\pi}} \cdot \frac{\sqrt{\varepsilon}}{T_e^{3/2}} \cdot \exp\left(-\frac{\varepsilon}{T_e}\right)
\]

%The electron number density per unit energy at energy \( \varepsilon \) is:

\[
n_e(\varepsilon) = n_{e,\text{total}} \cdot f(\varepsilon)
\]
